🔒 Privacy Policy

Snap Sieve
collects nothing.

Last updated: April 15, 2026 · Effective for all versions of Snap Sieve (iOS & macOS)

🛡️

Your photos never leave your device. Snap Sieve makes zero network calls. All AI processing uses Apple-native frameworks — Vision, Core ML, and Core Image. No data is collected, transmitted, or stored externally. Period.

Data We Collect

None. Snap Sieve does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, photos, or any other information from your device.

How It Works (On-Device Only)

  • All photo analysis is performed locally using Apple's Vision and Core ML frameworks.
  • No network requests are made at any point during app usage.
  • No third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.
  • Photos accessed via PhotoKit remain under your iCloud/Photos library — Snap Sieve only reads them for local analysis.

Third-Party Services

None. Snap Sieve does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or data processors.

Children's Privacy

Since no data is collected, Snap Sieve is safe for users of all ages. It fully complies with COPPA and equivalent regulations.
